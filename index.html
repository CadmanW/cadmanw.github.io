About me

    Hi, my name is Cadman Warren. I am from Cookeville, Tennessee. I always wanted to be creative, but never found the right tool to use my creativity. First, it was art, then it was making mods for games, and from there I started to really like computers. So I tried to learn software development for games, struggled and quit many many times, and eventually gave up. I forgot about my love for computers until I came across web development. So far, it has been my favorite thing to do besides skateboarding! In my free time, I'm either learning more about software development, practicing making websites, playing video games, or skateboarding.
